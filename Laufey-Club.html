Who is Laufey?

Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir, (pron. lay-vay) is a 2 time grammy nominated, and winner, and Chinese-Icelandic singer-songwriter, composer and musician. She has released 2 EP's (Typical of Me and A Very Laufey Holiday), and three albums, two of them grammy winning albums (Everything I Know About Love, Bewitched, A Matter of Time.)
